Who we are:

Munyangane Itani Incorporated (MI inc.) is a small size law firm established in 2021, by Mufhati Munyangane. After working as an associate attorney for four years in one of the medium sized law firms in Pretoria, the founder of MI inc. took a conscious decision to branch out, with a wealth of skills he acquired as an associate attorney.

When one is dedicated and invests a lot of time in learning the traits of fraternity, he begins to see areas which can either be improved or where there is a shortage of people to provide quality service. That is what the founder of Munyangane Itani Incorporated had in mind when he ventured into starting a law firm.

He wanted to use his wealth of experience and exceptional expertise – ranging from commercial and corporate advisory matters, to complex and high-level litigation cases – to bring about a different, team-centred approach to the world of legal practice.

Do not be fooled by the small size of this firm, as one of the foundations upon which the practice is being built is “growth”. We believe in growth, and after all, all things start small, and everything has a beginning.

“Do not despise the days of small beginnings.”
